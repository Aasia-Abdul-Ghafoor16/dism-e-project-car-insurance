Who We Are

Pakistan's Premier Vehicle Insurance Platform

SafeCarz Insurance was founded with a single mission: to make quality vehicle insurance accessible, affordable, and simple for every Pakistani. From the bustling streets of Karachi to the highways of Lahore, we protect what matters most — your family's safety.

Headquartered in Saudabad Malir, Karachi, we have grown to partner with the world's leading automotive brands — spanning both four-wheelers and two-wheelers — to deliver insurance solutions tailored for Pakistan's unique road conditions.
